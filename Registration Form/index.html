PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE COMPLETING THIS FORM

  1. Read the Yearbook as well as the Prospectus carefully before completing and submitting this Registration Form with all the relevant information.
  2. The IMM Graduate School does not endorse third parties who offer tuition on IMM Graduate School programmes. Should an IMM Graduate School student enrol with any third party for tuition on IMM Graduate School programmes, the IMM Graduate School will be entitled to suspend the student’s registration with the IMM Graduate School.
  3. New students who meet the IMM Graduate School entry requirements, will only be registered once required payment as well as all required documents have been received.
  4. The IMM Graduate School cannot accept/process incomplete Registration Forms, even if required payment has been received. The onus is on the student to provide all outstanding information/documentation in order for the IMM Graduate School to process the Registration Form.
  5. Late registration fees will apply when the Registration Form is received after the due date, even if the payment was received by the due date.
  6. Where reference is made to “Certified Copies”, photocopies of the documents must bear an original stamp and signature of a Commissioner of Oaths.

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY DECLARATION BY THE STUDENT I hereby acknowledge and agree to the Academic Integrity Statement as set out below and further acknowledge that this Academic Integrity Statment applies for the duration of my studies at the IMM Graduate School:

  1. I will not recieve nor will I elicit any assisstance from/to another student in the process of completion of any assessment.
  2. I understand that academic discissions with other students are encouraged, but not to provide answers to assessment questions in the process of completion of the assessment. Therefore my assessment answers will be entirely my own work and not the work of other students, regardless of any academic discissions I was a part of.
  3. I will not plagiarise the work of others and submit it as my own, nor will I self-plagiarise using the excerpts from work I may have submitted previously for assessment whether at the IMM Graduate School oor any other institution. I recognise that I am responsible for understanding and applying the provisions of the IMM Graduate School Policy on Plagiarism Prevention.
  4. I will not outsource any outstanding work to a third party by allowing someone else to contribute to or complete assessments on my behalf.
  5. I understand and acknowledge that any act of academic dishonesty may result in academic misconduct procedures being instituted against me to its full extent as prescribed by IMM Graduate School policies. If found to have breached the IMM Graduate School policies, I will submit to the academic inquiry process and acknowledge that an outcome of this process may lead to me recieving zero percent for an assessment, or even that I may be disenrolled.
